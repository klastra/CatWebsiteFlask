About Boo

Boo is a Domestic short-haired cat from Sunnyvale, California. He is currently three years old.

He is a rescue from San Jose Animal Shelter, and was adopted by Kirsty Lastra. He was four months old when he was adopted.

Now, Boo is a big, shy, cat that becomes playful once he is comfortable with you. He likes to play fetch, and run around the patio.

Boo's Likes and Dislikes
